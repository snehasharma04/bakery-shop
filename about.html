About Us!

WELCOME TO cakeNbake!

Welcome to cakeNbake, where every bite tells a story! We are a delightful bakery nestled in the heart of Banasthali Vidyapith, founded with a passion for creating decadent treats that tantalize the taste buds and warm the soul. Our journey began with a simple desire to spread joy through the art of baking, and today, we continue to uphold that tradition with every confection we craft.

Mission: At cakeNbake, our mission is to sweeten life's moments by providing freshly baked goods made with love and care. We aim to exceed our customers' expectations with every bite, ensuring that each treat is a delightful experience worth savoring.

Vision: Our vision is to become the go-to destination for indulgent desserts and baked delights in Banasthali Vidyapith and beyond. We strive to foster a community where people can come together to celebrate special occasions, share laughter, and create cherished memories over delicious treats.

History: Founded by a BTech student at Banasthali Vidyapith, cakeNbake started as a humble hostel-based venture, baking goods for friends and family. Over time, word spread about our delectable creations, and our small operation blossomed into a beloved local bakery. Today, we are proud to serve our community with a wide array of baked goods, each one crafted with the same dedication and love that inspired our beginnings.

Meet Our Team: Behind every delicious treat at cakeNbake is a team of talented individuals who share a common love for baking and a commitment to excellence. From our skilled pastry chefs to our friendly customer service representatives, each member of our team plays a vital role in bringing our vision to life. Together, we strive to create memorable experiences for our customers and uphold the highest standards of quality and service.

Our Promise: At cakeNbake, we believe that every moment is worth celebrating, and we are dedicated to helping you make those moments even sweeter. Whether you're indulging in one of our signature cakes, treating yourself to a freshly baked pastry, or sharing a box of cookies with loved ones, we promise to deliver exceptional taste and unparalleled quality in every bite.

Thank you for choosing cakeNbake. We look forward to serving you soon!
